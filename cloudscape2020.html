The Cloudscape 2020

"The Cloudscape 2020" is a music project I worked on with a small group of close friends in 2019. Within this project, I contributed by helping the creation of a few beats for several tracks and handling the design of the album cover. The completion of all songs required a dedicated timeline of approximately six months. The album's profound emotive impact served as a catalyst for the creation of its accompanying cover artwork.
