DJ Slushii

Slushii is an American musician, DJ, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He reached out to me back in 2019 and asked for an artwork to promote his new song "Dreaming of You".
Slushii was so happy with the illustration that he quickly approached me for another commission. This one was requested to be illustrated in the style of the movie "Akira".
